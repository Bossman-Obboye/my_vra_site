VRA International Schools

Nurturing future leaders through a holistic and globally-focused education, fostering academic excellence and character development.

Our Identity

VRA International Schools are committed to providing a world-class educational experience that extends beyond academics. We focus on developing well-rounded individuals equipped with critical thinking skills, global awareness, and a strong sense of community responsibility.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic and globally-focused education that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and character development, preparing students to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading international school in Ghana, recognized for innovative teaching, diverse learning experiences, and producing impactful future leaders.

Our Programs & Impact

Discover the diverse educational programs and initiatives that define our commitment to nurturing every student's potential.

Early Childhood & Primary Education

  • Foundational Learning: Providing a strong academic foundation in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking from an early age.
  • Play-Based Learning: Incorporating engaging play-based methodologies to foster creativity, social skills, and a love for learning.
  • Safe & Nurturing Environment: Creating a supportive and secure environment where young learners can thrive and explore.

Comprehensive Secondary Education

  • Rigorous Curriculum: Offering a challenging curriculum that prepares students for higher education and future careers.
  • Subject Specialization: Providing a wide range of subjects and electives to cater to diverse interests and academic pathways.
  • University Preparation: Guiding students through university application processes and preparing them for academic success at the tertiary level.

Holistic Development & Extracurriculars

  • Arts & Culture: Encouraging participation in visual arts, music, drama, and cultural activities to foster creativity and expression.
  • Sports & Athletics: Promoting physical well-being and teamwork through various sports programs and athletic competitions.
  • Leadership & Clubs: Offering opportunities for leadership development through student government, clubs, and societies.

Fostering Global Citizenship

  • Intercultural Understanding: Promoting awareness and appreciation of diverse cultures and global perspectives.
  • Community Service: Engaging students in local and international community service projects to instill social responsibility.
  • Environmental Awareness: Educating students on environmental sustainability and encouraging eco-friendly practices.
