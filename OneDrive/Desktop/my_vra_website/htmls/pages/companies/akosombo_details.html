Akosombo Hotels Limited

Your premier destination for unparalleled service, culture, and memorable experiences.

Our Identity

We are a prominent hospitality entity contributing to Ghana's tourism sector. We are committed to providing essential services to visitors and the local community, operating in close association with VRA's broader community development and asset management initiatives.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality services, offering comfortable accommodation, quality dining, and memorable experiences for guests, while promoting local culture and sustainable tourism practices.

Our Vision

To be the premier hospitality destination in Ghana, recognized for its unparalleled service, unique cultural offerings, and commitment to guest satisfaction and community enrichment.

Our Impact & Services

Excellence in Hospitality

  • Accommodation Services: Offering a range of lodging options, from standard rooms to suites, catering to diverse guest needs including leisure travelers, business delegates, and tourists.
  • Dining & Catering: Operating restaurants and bars that offer a variety of local and international cuisine, as well as providing professional catering services for all types of events.

Premier Event & Conference Facilities

We provide modern venues and comprehensive services for conferences, workshops, corporate retreats, and social events. Our facilities are equipped to support both local and international gatherings, ensuring every event is a success.

Promoting Local Tourism & Recreation

  • Recreational Amenities: Enhancing the guest experience with facilities such as swimming pools, fitness centers, and curated access to local attractions like boat tours on the Volta Lake and visits to the Akosombo Dam.
  • Local Tourism Promotion: Actively participating in and promoting tourism activities in the Akosombo area and the broader Volta Region, highlighting Ghana's natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Commitment to Community & Economy

  • Job Creation: Providing significant employment opportunities for the local population in a wide variety of rewarding hospitality roles.
  • Community Support: Engaging in initiatives that benefit the local community, including sourcing local produce, supporting local artisans, and participating actively in community development projects.
