Our Management Team

Meet the dedicated leaders steering the Volta River Authority towards a sustainable and prosperous energy future for Ghana.

Leading With Purpose, Delivering Results

At the heart of VRA's operations is a leadership team comprised of experienced engineers, strategists, administrators, and innovators committed to excellence in power generation, sustainability, and national development.

Executive Leadership

👤

Ing. Edward Ekow Obeng-Kenzo

Ag. Chief Executive, VRA

A seasoned mechanical engineer and administrator with 24 years of experience in the power sector. He has held several key roles in thermal generation and engineering management. He holds a BSc in Mechanical Engineering and an MPA from KNUST. He is a member of the Ghana Institution of Engineering.
